In long words

Anime is a Japanese animation style that's often used in films and television series. It's characterized by: Colorful graphics, Vibrant characters, Fantastical themes, Expressive characters, Relatively limited animation. Anime is often hand-drawn or computer-generated. It often combines action-packed plots with stark, colorful graphics. The term "anime" is a Japanese abbreviation for "animation". In Japan, the word "anime" can refer to any kind of animation. Outside of Japan, however, "anime" usually refers to animation that's produced in Japan. Anime often works alongside manga, which are Japanese comic books. Anime can bring manga stories to life with animated television series.
